Gulab Jamun

Description

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk (approximately)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add milk gradually and knead the mixture into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small equal-sized portions and roll them into smooth balls without any cracks.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Gently slide the prepared dough balls into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown.
  6. Meanwhile, in another pan, prepare the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water together until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  7. Add cardamom powder to the sugar syrup and turn off the heat.
  8. Remove the fried dough balls from oil and immediately dip them into the warm sugar syrup.
  9. Let the Gulab Jamuns soak in the syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  10. Garnish with chopped nuts if desired and serve warm or at room temperature.
