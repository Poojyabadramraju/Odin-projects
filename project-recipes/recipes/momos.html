Momos

Description

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, bell peppers, etc.), finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour with water to make a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by saut√©ing onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into thin circles.
  5. Place a spoonful of the vegetable filling in the center of each circle.
  6. Pinch and fold the edges of the circle to seal the momos into a half-moon shape.
  7. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes until cooked.
  8. Serve hot with dipping sauce of your choice.
