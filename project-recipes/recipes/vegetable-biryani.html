Vegetable Biryani

Description

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, cauliflower, etc.)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste>
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)

Preparation

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add mixed vegetables, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. In a separate pot, bring water to boil. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  8. In a bowl, whisk yogurt and biryani masala together.
  9. Layer the vegetable mixture in a heavy-bottomed pot. Top with a layer of cooked rice.
  10. Sprinkle chopped cilantro and mint leaves over the rice layer.
  11. Drizzle the yogurt mixture evenly over the rice.
  12. Repeat the layers until all the rice and vegetable mixture are used up, ending with a rice layer on top.
  13. If using, sprinkle saffron milk over the rice.
  14. Cover the pot tightly with a lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and flavors are absorbed.
  15. Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Dum Biryani!
